Real-World Latency

A "Page Fault" (MISS) forces the CPU to wait for the SSD/HDD. This takes ~5-10ms, while a "Hit" takes <10ns. Efficient algorithms like LRU (used in Linux/MacOS) reduce these penalties drastically.

Industrial Use

LRU: Used in database caching (Redis) and browser history management.
Optimal: Theoretical maximum performance; used by researchers to evaluate new algorithms.
